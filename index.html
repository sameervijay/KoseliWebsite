What We Do

Koseli Foundation is an NGO that operates Koseli School, a center for children living in slums in Kathmandu, Nepal.
The school was named Koseli, which means "a gift," because it offers students the gift of education, along with food and clothing.

Sushma lives in a slum with her aunt and uncle because her parents, both alcoholics, were physically abusive.
